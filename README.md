# privacy-policy
Privacy policy for Trekking Sites Nepal
At Trekking Sites Nepal, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our trekking booking application.

1. Introduction
This application is designed to facilitate trekking bookings in Nepal. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect
We collect user information that you voluntarily provide through our booking forms, including:

Full Name
Email Address
Phone Number / WhatsApp Number
Booking Preferences (Trek selection, dates, etc.)
3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:

Process your booking requests.
Send booking notifications via WhatsApp to our trekking service providers to coordinate your trip.
Communicate with you regarding your trek details and availability.
4. Data Protection
We are committed to ensuring your data is secure.

We do not sell or distribute your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
We do not misuse your personal data.
Your information is shared only with the necessary service providers required to facilitate your trekking experience.
5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: Abhaya@trainspace.com
